Frequently Asked Questions

1. Where is Scoots located?

Scoots is nestled on the picturesque shores of Cozumel, ensuring convenient access for ferry arrivals and cruise ship passengers alike.

2. What types of rentals does Scoots offer?

Scoots offers a variety of rentals, including Honda motor scooters perfect for solo jaunts, rugged Jeeps for exploring the island's terrain, and side-by-side ATVs designed for group adventures.

3. How can I book a rental with Scoots?

Booking a rental with Scoots is easy! You can make a reservation online through our website or visit one of our conveniently located rental spots on Cozumel.

4. What sets Scoots apart from other rental companies?

At Scoots, we go beyond rentals to provide gateways to boundless exploration. We offer a diverse fleet of vehicles catering to every traveler's whims, ensuring that you can uncover Cozumel's hidden gems on your terms.

5. Is there a minimum age requirement for renting with Scoots?

Yes, the minimum age requirement for renting with Scoots varies depending on the type of vehicle. Please refer to our rental terms and conditions for more information.
